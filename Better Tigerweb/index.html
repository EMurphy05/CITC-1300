Tired of Tigerweb being so complicated?

Heres a guide!

Tigerweb, the official website for chattanooga state community college, is notorious for being not so user friendly. The site is hard to navigate, to the point where most students cannot find their class schedules without someone elses helping to show them how. The site is also outdated, with most pages being basic text links with no style or formatting, and many linked pages that no longer exist, are not actually used for what they say they do, or just have not been updated in a very long time.

Tigerweb Self-service

The Self-Service tab holds most of the more important information and tools you will be using in Tigerweb, but it is quite complicated to navigate. To access your schedule, class registration, financial standings, personal information, or pay your tuition, go to Self-Service in yout Tigerweb. Student records and administration are under the "Student" link. Your class schedule is also in there under the "Registration" link, and then "Week at a Glance"

Elearn

Elearn is the Google Classroom of chatt state, where most professors put class assignments, reasources, grades, and feedback.

Tiger Tracks

Tiger Tracks goes to a third party site called Degreeworks, so it is much easier to navigate, but this is where you can see your A number, current GPA, and degree information.

Tigerpedia
