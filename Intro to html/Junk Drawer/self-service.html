Class Schedule

  1. Open self-service by pressing the tiger image in your Tigerweb
  2. Open the second to last link that says "Student"
  3. Open the middle link that says "Regestration"
  4. Open the fourth from the top link that says "Week at a Glance"

If you have difficulties reading the schedule, look near the bottom of the blue blocks of text that represent your classes. The bottom two sentances give you the times of the class and the room numbers.

Sign up for Classes

Note: you will need information from Tiger Tracks and your student pin, which you can get from contacting your advisor.
  1. Open Self-service from Tigerweb
  2. From the Main Menu of Self Service, click "Student" then "Registration" then "Look Up Classes".
  3. Select the correct term. (Note: TCAT is the Tennessee College of Applied Technology. All other student should chose a Credit term.)
  4. Near the top, right under "Credit Students:" press the link in "add classes with Schedule Planner"
  5. You'll then get to choose some options such as which campus you want to attend and if you want to include short term (condensed schedule) classes.
  6. Next, add all the courses you know you need to take (which can be figured out in Tiger tracks). Click Add Course and then search for each course by Subject area and Course number, or by Course Attributes (such as "Natural Phys Science with Lab"), or by Instructor (if you have a preferred instructor). After finding each course, select it and click Add Course.
