Steps

  • The first thing you will need to do is log in to your Tigerweb
  • Next, click on the image of a tiger to go to the "Self-service" page
  • From here, press the "Student" link
  • Now you need to press the link that says "Registration"
  • Lastly, press on "Student Detail Schedule"

What am I looking at?

Good question. You should be at a page full of text that all sort of blurs together. Luckily, almost everything is labeled fairly well, and is rather easy to figure out. Just keep in mind that the grey bars with info are referring to the block of text above.

Also, Chatt State lists different days with abbreviations, so here is a key. Please also note that they seem to not have spaces between, so where you see TR, that means the class is on Tuesday and Thursday.

  • M = Monday
  • T = Tuesday
  • W = Wednesday
  • R = Thursday
  • F = Friday
  • S = Saturday
